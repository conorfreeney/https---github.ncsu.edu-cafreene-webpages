Welcome to my about me page!

Hi! My name is Conor Freeney, and my favorite place to walk around campus is from my dorm to Pullen Park. I live in Lee Residence Hall, so it is a pretty lengthy distance. I am a birder, and recently I have enjoyed observing blue jays, Eastern bluebirds, and Northern Cardinals on and nearby campus. When I get to Pullen Park, I either like to paint with watercolors or sit and read a book.
